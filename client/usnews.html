For the fourth consecutive year, MCLA has been named a Top Ten Public Liberal Arts College by the U.S. News & World Report.

MCLA operates on a rolling admission basis with decisions for fall and spring terms commencing in early December and continuing through the summer. Apply today!

Fall semester candidates are encuorages to submit applications by the Match 1st priority deadline for full consideration for financial aid and scholarships.

The college strives to enroll qualified students from Massachusetts and the nation, who exibit the potential for academic and personal growth, and will benefit from a liberal arts education.

As a member of the Massachusetts Public Higher Education System, MCLA's admissions standards are determined by the Massachusetts Department of Higher Education.

To ensure consideration for admission, housing, and classroom space, students are advised to apply early.

Interviews are not required, but highly recommended, to schedule, please contact the Office of Admission.
